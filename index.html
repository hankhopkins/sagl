How Skins Work
Skins Pot
$800

Gross vs Net
⛳
Par 3s & 5s use gross scores; par 4s use net (half-strokes apply). This balances the odds between low and high handicappers.

League Basics
📋Format
9-hole match play. Each player carries a USGA GHIN handicap. 10 points per match (1 per hole + 1 bonus for match winner, 0.5 each if tied). Low vs. low, high vs. high.
½Half-Strokes
Half-strokes are used to reduce ties and minimize rounding luck. Differential mapped using the USGA Course Handicap tool. E.g., 3.34–3.66 = 3.5 strokes.
📱Commissioner Notifications
Notify commissioner before your round (tee time, location, strokes, tees). Submit scorecard ASAP after round. Denote which tees you played from.
🔢Tiebreakers
Within a season: (1) head-to-head record, (2) coin flip. Playoff seeding: regular season standings → head-to-head → combined S1+S2 points → coin flip.
Scramble Week (Week 4)
⛳Handicap
Team hdcp = 35% of lower index + 15% of higher index. E.g., A=10, B=20 → (10×0.35)+(20×0.15) = 6.5.
📍Ball Placement
Off green: within one driver-length, no closer to hole, same area. On green: within one putter-head length of original spot.
✓Gimmes
Any putt within one putter-length (~3 ft) is conceded. No putter greater than 36" to measure. Can't concede par while birdie putt from same spot is pending.
🚫Other Rules
All OB played as penalty area. No mulligans. Eagle Pot does not apply; Team of the Week does. Takes place Week 4 to avoid affecting playoff qualification.
Playoffs Format
6Teams Qualify
Top 3 from Spring Season + Top 3 from Summer Season automatically qualify. Remaining spots filled by wild card (combined points).
↑Seeds 1 & 2 Bye
Top two seeds receive a first-round bye. Higher seed presents two course options; opponent chooses.
⛳Playoff Rounds
Each round is 9 or 18 holes at mutual discretion. Previous champions: Manske/Wesling ('23), Leadley/Mullins ('24), Hopkins/Mugford ('25).
Special Rules
🥞Breakfast Balls (New 2026)
One free re-tee on hole 1 only. Must use second ball if taken. Both players must agree before teeing off. Hole 1 is ineligible for skins when breakfast balls are in play.
📊Handicap Adjustments (New 2026)
All 9-hole league rounds in which the player shot their handicap or better will result in a −1.3 score differential adjustment from the GHIN calculation. For example, if course par is 36 and the player shoots a net 36 or better, their GHIN score differential is subtracted by 1.3. This better reflects the competitiveness and future predictive value of each player's handicap.
❌Unfinished Rounds
If 7+ holes completed, both teams may agree to end early. Unplayed holes scored as tied net pars. Each team gets +1 stroke for TOTW fairness.
🚪Forfeits
Non-forfeiting player gets full victory point. Forfeiting player: ghost score = course hdcp + 5 strokes (if index <13.0) or +6 strokes (if ≥13.0).
